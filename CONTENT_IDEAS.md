### 7. FAQ Section

**Q: How long does 'nduja last?**
A: Unopened: 6 months refrigerated. Opened: 2-3 weeks in fridge.

**Q: Is it really that spicy?**
A: Classic = medium heat (like jalapeño). Mild = gentle warmth. Extra Hot = habanero level.

**Q: Can I freeze it?**
A: Yes! Freezes beautifully for up to 6 months.

**Q: What's the texture?**
A: Soft and spreadable at room temp, firm when chilled.

**Q: Is it gluten-free?**
A: Yes! Just pork, peppers, and spices.

**Q: Vegan/vegetarian option?**
A: Not currently, but we're researching plant-based alternatives!

**Q: Where do you ship?**
A: All 50 states! Express shipping available.

**Q: Bulk/wholesale pricing?**
